AUGMENTED RADIOLOGY

Brainscores is a novel AI powered medical image analysis platform to revolutionize diganostics for the most common brain disorders. Developed by experts in medical imaging, AI, and software engineering, Brainscores will provide unprecedented ways for medical doctors, engineers, and data scientists to team up in solving each patient's medical needs.

WHERE WE'RE AT

Our prototype is currently being developed, hand in hand with clinical and AI experts. We are confident to launch the usuability trials in summer 2023. In the next 12 months, we will validate the platform with our partners.

WHAT'S UNDER THE HOOD

Our platform provides an easy to use interface that leverages the following modules:

  • MultiStream: Multimodal data processing (T1-weighted, FLAIR, Diffusion, Microstructure, Functional)
  • MultiFuse: Multimodal data integration into a common view
  • BrainScorer: Probability based image diagnostic
  • AIAssist: AI technology to highlight significant deviations
  • CaseReporter: Automated radiological report generator
  • NeuroNavegation: Support to download and use our results with neurosurgery assisted hardware
